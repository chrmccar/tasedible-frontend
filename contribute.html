🌱 Contribute a New Plant

Thanks for helping us grow the TasEdible database! Here's how to add a new plant:

  1. Open the editable spreadsheet:
    📄 Open Google Sheet
  2. Fill out a new row with details of the plant.
  3. Upload a photo of the plant:
    📷 Upload Photo to Google Drive
  4. After uploading, right-click the image → “Get link” → Copy the shareable Google Drive link.
  5. Paste that link into the image_url column in the spreadsheet.

That's it! Your plant will appear in the public list once it's saved.
